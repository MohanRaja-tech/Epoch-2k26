Paper Survival

Present your ideas

Team Size 2-3 Members
Duration 9:30am-11:00am
Difficulty High

Paper Presentation is an academic and technical event where participants present original or review-based research work on a chosen topic. The objective is to evaluate the participant's depth of understanding, clarity of ideas, analytical ability, and presentation skills. Participants prepare a paper in standard format and present it using slides within a specified time limit, followed by a question-and-answer session with judges.

Rules & Requirements

  • Maximum page limit typically 6–8
  • Plagiarism must be minimal
  • Time limit usually 8–12 minutes for presentation
  • 2–5 minutes for Q&A
  • Be on venue on time

Binary Battle

Code in different way

Team Size 2 Members
Duration 10:30am-11:30am
Difficulty Medium

The Coding Event is a time-bound competitive programming contest designed to test problem-solving ability, logical thinking, and coding skills. Participants are required to solve algorithmic and logical problems using their preferred programming language within a limited duration. Accuracy, optimal solutions, and efficient time management play a major role in securing the top positions.

Rules & Requirements

  • Languages: C,C++,JAVA,Python
  • Score based on number of correct solutions and total time taken
  • No internet access during contest
  • Decision of judges and coordinators will be final
  • Any malpractice results in immediate elimination
