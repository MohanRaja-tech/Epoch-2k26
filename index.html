About EPOCH 2026

Welcome to the Ultimate Challenge

EPOCH 2026 is not just another technical symposium - it's a battleground where innovation meets competition. Inspired by the intensity and strategy of survival games, our event challenges participants to push their limits, think creatively, and emerge victorious.

Over the course of two thrilling days, students from colleges across the nation will compete in technical and non-technical events, each designed to test different aspects of their abilities. From coding marathons to creative challenges, every event is a game, and every participant is a player.
